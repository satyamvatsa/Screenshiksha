Choosing Us!

Screen Shiksha is an EdTech venture aiming to change how creative arts, Film making, Animation, VFX , editing and Multimedia are learn and taught globally. It is a curated training & networking program for visionary Creative people from around the globe who dares to dream.

Our mission is to raise awareness of media, arts, and multimedia education throughout the world and make it accessible to as many aspirants as possible by using the digital arena to reach every corner and nook. We hope to create a supportive environment where students' creative abilities can be developed through engaging online courses and networking opportunities with members of the media and arts sector, improving their employability.
